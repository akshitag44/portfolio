I have aquired that certain skill, and I can prove that claim from my tutoring experience. Over the summer, I tutored first-graders in math, including addition, subtraction, and more. For us, all of these concepts are really basic in the world of mathematics, but for first-graders, this was a very new concept and almost foreign to them. This mean they often forget learned concepts. Because of that, I had to have a lot of patience teaching, and I had to be understanding. The kids also would not want to do work at times, and this would show me being as cooperative.
