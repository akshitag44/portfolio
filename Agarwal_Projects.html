Project: Space Invaders

Course: Computer Science Principles

Duration: August 30, 2018 - September 6, 2018

When my partner and I got handed our MIT App Inventor Project, we immediately chose the game Space Invaders. Space Invaders is a retro arcade game from the late 70s. The goal of the game is to shoot all the aliens every round while they come closer and closer to you. Once they touch you, the game is over. While working on this project, my partner and I split the work in half, and we distributed all of it. We both did documentation, we both did the coding, and we both worked hard on the project. While working on this project, the game presented a lot of different problems, and through this, I learned a new way to approach solutions and boost creativity. Both my partner and I approached one problem in a million different ways, and I feel like I benefited from this project because it forced me to think creatively.
