About Me

My name is Akshit Agarwal. I am currently a freshman at Dublin High School. I have a lot of hobbies like creating music, playing sports, or writing out stories. I've been interested in Computer Science ever since I was a little kid, when my love for video games started. As I played classic Nintendo games like Super Mario Sunshine and Donkey Kong, I always had wondered on how these games were actually made. As I grew older and older, I became more and more interested in the field of Computer Science. While I grew, my passions for basketball and creating music had taken off, as I spent my time practicing and learning, but I still never lost that wonder for Computer Science. Now that I am in the class, I am excited for what's to come and I look forward to a year of coding!
